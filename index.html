I am an Associate Professor at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

My research interests are in representation theory with applications in geometry, topology and mathematical physics.

Research

Here you can find my major research topics and main publications (see also Full Publication List):
  • Enumerative geometry, quantum integrable systems and q-Langlands correspondence
  • Higher Teichmüller theory
  • Quantum groups and Kac-Moody algebras
  • Homotopy Gerstenhaber algebras, Einstein equations and Courant algebroids
  • Geometric Langlands correspondence for supergroups
  • Homotopy algebras of topological conformal field theories
  • Integrable systems and (super)conformal field theory

  • Higher Teichmüller theory
    We construct the analogue of Penner coordinates on the N=1 and N=2 super-Teichmüller spaces. Future projects based on this work involve super-analogue of cluster algebras, computations of volumes of supermoduli spaces, quantization of (higher) super-Teichmüller spaces.
